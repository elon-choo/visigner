Pricing questions

How does annual billing save 20%?

Annual plans are billed once a year at the discounted seat rate — effectively two months free. Pro is $39/seat monthly or $31/seat on annual ($372/seat/year), a $96 saving per seat.

What happens when I add or remove a seat?

Seats are prorated to the day. Add one mid-cycle and you are charged only for the remaining days; remove one and the credit rolls to your next invoice.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes — upgrade or downgrade anytime from the billing page. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the end of the current period so you keep what you have paid for.

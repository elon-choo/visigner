Questions, answered

Does it work offline?

Yes. Marrow is local-first — every session is written to a file on your machine and syncs only when you choose to.

Which shells are supported?

bash, zsh and fish out of the box, with completions. PowerShell support is in beta.

Can I export my data if I leave?

mar export --json hands you everything. It is your data; we never lock it in.
